# Partnerships — inbound channel & targets

> When outreach grows, partners will want to piggyback. Have a front door open from day one.

---

## Design principle

Build so partners can **see themselves** in the product before the conversation happens:

- Footer: *"Partner with us"* → simple form or email
- "Supported by" slot on homepage (empty until first sponsor — shows intent)
- Referrer codes in URL (`?ref=`) — attribution ready
- Analytics hook (even manual) — "X users this month" for pitch decks
- Disclaimer + privacy page — required before any bank or agency talks seriously

---

## Inbound partnership channel (ship with v1)

**Page or form fields:**

| Field | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| Organization name | Who they are |
| Contact name + email | Follow-up |
| Partnership type | Sponsor · Affiliate · Referrer · Content · Other |
| What they're offering | Brief free text |
| What they want | Logo · Co-marketing · Data insights · Distribution · Other |

**Auto-reply template:**
> Thanks for reaching out. We review partnership requests weekly. This tool is free for all users — we only partner with orgs that align with that mission.

**Where it lives:** `/partners` page or link in app footer → Google Form is fine for v0.

**Email alias:** `partners@yourdomain.ca` (or Gmail forwarder until domain live).

## IRCC / Discover Canada outreach (aspirational playbook)

**Do not claim affiliation.** Ever. Until written agreement exists.

**Phase 1 — Earn legitimacy (0–5K users)**
- Source all content from Discover Canada
- Prominent disclaimer
- Collect pass testimonials with permission
- Share tool in library/settlement channels IRCC already trusts

**Phase 2 — Document impact (5K–10K users)**
- One-page impact report: users, modules completed, mock pass rates, geographic spread
- Offer free license to IRCC for internal reference (no money — pure goodwill)

**Phase 3 — Formal outreach (10K+ users)**
- Contact: IRCC communications / public outreach (not application processing)
- Pitch: *"Independent free digital companion to Discover Canada — used by X future Canadians. Happy to discuss listing on canada.ca resources or co-promotion during Citizenship Week."*
- Ask: resource page link, not endorsement
- Parallel: Library and Archives Canada, Institute for Canadian Citizenship

**What IRCC might actually give:** Link on a resources page. Mention in settlement worker materials. Never "official partner" — but **distribution at scale**.

## Legal guardrails

1. **PIPEDA** — consent checkbox on any form; privacy page published
2. **No raw PII sales** — partnerships use aggregated insights or on-platform ads only
3. **Affiliate disclosure** — visible on any monetized recommendation
4. **RCIC rules** — referrers cannot imply IRCC endorsement or guaranteed pass
5. **Bank partnerships** — compliance review on copy before launch
