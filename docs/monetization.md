# Monetization — partners pay, not users

> We deserve to make real money — but only after serving the community. If we don't, the tool stays free anyway.

Individuals get **full prep forever free**. Revenue covers hosting (~$0–20/mo) and optional time investment through layers below.

---

## Revenue layers (priority order)

| Layer | Who pays | When to activate | Year 1 realistic |
|-------|----------|------------------|------------------|
| **0 — Free core** | Nobody | Day 1 | $0 (this is the product) |
| **1 — Tips / support** | Grateful users (optional) | Day 1 | $200–2,000 |
| **2 — Referrer cross-sell** | Banks, telcos (affiliate) | 500+ users | $0–5,000 |
| **3 — B2B dashboard** | RCICs, settlement agencies | 1,000+ users | $0–10,000 |
| **4 — Sponsorship** | Brand keeps it free | 3,000+ users | $0–25,000 |
| **5 — Consulting** | Orgs wanting civic UX | Anytime | $5,000–50,000 |
| **6 — Strategic exit** | Acquirer | 15,000+ users | Speculative |

**Day 1–90 focus:** Layers 0 + 1 + open partnership channel (see [partnerships.md](partnerships.md)).

---

## Layer 1 — Tips & optional support

**Mechanic:** Footer link — *"This tool is free. If it helped you, buy me a coffee."* Ko-fi, Stripe Payment Link, or PayPal.

**Optional "supporter" tier (not paywall):**
- Same full access as everyone
- Supporters get: name on thank-you page (opt-in), early access to new modules, nothing essential gated

**Why this works:** Reddit and newcomer communities respect optional support. ApnaToronto stays free; grateful users still donate.

---

## Layer 2 — Affiliate cross-sell (users never pay us)

Show **after** mock test pass or module completion — at the *opportunity* moment, not the *anxiety* moment.

| Partner type | User value | Typical affiliate |
|--------------|------------|-------------------|
| Newcomer banking (RBC, Scotiabank, TD) | First account as new citizen | $50–200 / funded account |
| Remittance (Wise, Remitly) | Sending money home | $10–25 / signup |
| CELPIP / language prep | Failed language requirement | Varies |
| Passport / travel checklist | Free guide + affiliate optional | Low |

**Referrer commission:** RCICs earn on cross-sell when client uses their link — not on charging the client for prep.

**Ethics:** Clear disclosure. Only recommend things genuinely useful post-citizenship. Never sell data.

| Sponsor type | What they get | Fee range |
|--------------|---------------|-----------|

**Pitch when:** 3,000+ engaged users + pass testimonials + clean PIPEDA story.

## Layer 5 — Consulting & career brand

The app is a **portfolio piece** for civic / slow-tech work:

- Provincial digital services ("modernize X intake form")
- Nonprofit IRCC-adjacent tooling
- GovTech advisory retainer
- Behavioral interview story + LinkedIn authority

**Rate target:** $150–250/hr or project-based $10K–75K.

This layer can exceed all product revenue combined.

## Cost floor (stay lean)

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|
| Static hosting | $0 (Vercel/Netlify free tier) |
| Domain `.ca` | ~$15/year |
| Email (transactional) | $0–20/mo at scale |
| Google Form / spreadsheet | $0 |
| **Total until scale** | **< $50/month** |

Tips alone cover this at almost any usage level.

## Scenario summary (Year 1, net to you)

| Case | Users | Primary revenue | Net |
|------|-------|-----------------|-----|
| **Lowkey** | 300–800 | Tips only | $200–1,500 |
| **Expected** | 2,000–4,000 | Tips + 1 agency + affiliates starting | $4K–12K |
| **Surprisingly well** | 8K–15K | Sponsorship conversation + B2B + affiliates | $25K–60K |
| **WTF** | 25K+ | Sponsor deal + consulting contract or exit talk | $100K+ |

Full market for paid prep nationally: ~$300K–600K CAD/year across all competitors. **We are not competing for that slice.** We compete for trust, then monetize around the lifecycle.
